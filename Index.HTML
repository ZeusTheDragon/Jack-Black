HI!

Thomas Jacob "Jack" Black (born August 28, 1969) is an American actor, comedian, and musician. His live action films include High Fidelity (2000), Shallow Hal (2001), Orange County (2002), School of Rock (2003), Envy (2004), King Kong (2005), The Holiday (2006), Gulliver's Travels (2010), Bernie (2011), The House with a Clock in Its Walls (2018), and the Jumanji franchise. Black has also done voice acting for DreamWorks Animation's Kung Fu Panda franchise, The Super Mario Bros. Movie, and the video games Brutal Legend, Broken Age and Psychonauts 2. He is the lead vocalist of the band Tenacious D, and formed the duo in 1994 with long-time friend Kyle Gass. Since 2018, Black operates a YouTube channel called Jablinski Games.
